The first thing that come up in my mind when I wake up this morning is - What a life this has been. I opened my eyes, seeing the light rays coming from the half-opened blinds, closing my eyes again, trying to see myself if I was a spider that live on that dusty shelf, and asking - Can I leave instead.

Gie^[Soe Hok Gie] once wrote:

> Makhluk kecil kembalilah. Dari tiada ke tiada.
>
>Berbahagialah dalam ketiadaanmu.

That loosely translates to:

> Go back little one. Go back to nothingness.
>
> Be content in your nothingness.

And whenever Gie comes up, my mind goes to one of Yusi's^[Yusi Avianto Pareanom] short stories, the one when this coworker doesn't married or in some platonic relationship and the main character said "either you ... or you just have read too much Gie." [find this short story].

I know this is not a grand thing this struggle is. I know this is just something we encounter throughout this life. And yes it is difficult, and painfull enough to make me feel like a pathethic creature. But, hey, it's only live in my head, most of the time [I think I should refer to other notes when I sometimes feel like this things turns into real life things]. And also I think this is not something we should try to fix, or try to erase, but instead try to listen to.

Maybe it was lonely too. Like all of us.
